Questions, answered

Do I need any bartending experience? +

None at all. Every box includes illustrated, step-by-step recipe cards. Ingredients arrive pre-portioned, so you just pour, mix, and garnish.

Can I skip a month or cancel? +

Anytime, from your account — no fees, no phone calls. Skip a month, change your cadence, or cancel in a couple of clicks.

What's actually in each box? +

Premium spirits, fresh mixers, garnishes, illustrated recipe cards, and a rotating extra — enough to make 5–7 bar-quality cocktails.

Do I have to be 21+? +

Yes. You must be 21 or older to subscribe, and an adult signature is required on delivery. We currently ship to the lower 48 states.

When will my first box ship? +

Orders placed before the 20th ship at the start of the next month. You'll get tracking by email the moment it's on the way.
